## 1. Hook + Problem (~30 s)
We decided to tackle healthcare. Six million Canadians don't have a family doctor — the ones that do wait weeks to book and sit an hour before they're even seen. On average, Canadians wait **28.6 weeks** to get treated. Inside the clinic, staff are manually reviewing lab reports, calling patients one by one, and hoping they pick up — while doctors and front-desk lose hours to scheduling and paperwork. **1.4 million** Canadians are waiting on procedures right now, many because that follow-up call never got made. 
---

## 3. Solution (~15 s)

That's why we built **Clarus**. It gives doctors a single place to manage patient records and create fully customizable workflows. A lab report comes in — Clarus calls the patient, delivers a personalized message, and books the follow-up. Under 60 seconds. Zero staff involvement. And that's just one workflow. Doctors can build and tailor their own automations to fit exactly how their clinic runs — so they can spend less time on admin and more time with their patients.

## 5. Adding Patients from PDF (~10 s)

Everything uses secure local email/password authentication, so patient data stays protected. Doctors can add patients manually, but you can also just drop in a PDF of a medical record — Clarus parses it, updates existing patients or creates new ones automatically. Zero data entry.

---

## 6. Next Steps (~15 s)

Our next step is getting Clarus into clinics across Canada. More clinics on Clarus means doctors spend less time on admin and more time with patients — and Canadians stop waiting months for care that should take minutes. Thank you for listening and we'd love to answer any questions.
